Project: "HashFighter"

One of my favorite stages during any project is research. I knew I wanted the site to have a "cartoony" look. I also wanted it to resemble old boxing promotional posters. At this stage I try to list out any potential issues during development.

Concept

Two fighters are presented to the Twitter community where they are presented with specific hashtags to vote on. Each vote will increment a fighter's score box. First to 100 wins.

Issues

Utilizing the Twitter API is a tricky endeavor. One of issues were repeated tweets and the animation not being triggered promptly. The best solution I found was to increase Javascript's setInterval time which gave the program some buffer time to process.

Final

This project enable me to combined the things I love which is coding, designing, animation, and illustration.
